## The architectural finding

The CEO-095 brief assumed switching to `createServiceClient` would unblock ISR for both `/browse` and `/collections`. Two corrections from this session's investigation:

1. **/browse can never be page-level ISR** because it reads `searchParams` (filters, sort), which forces dynamic rendering in Next.js App Router regardless of `revalidate`. Right fix is data-layer caching (`unstable_cache`), which I implemented. Page stays dynamic; DB hit is cached server-side per filter combination.

2. **/collections is still dynamic at the edge** even after the data-layer fix. Verified post-deploy: `cache-control: no-cache, x-vercel-cache: MISS` on second hit. Root cause: `components/store/site-header.tsx` lines 10–11 call `createClient()` + `supabase.auth.getUser()` to render the auth-aware nav. SiteHeader is on every page, so every page reads cookies and opts out of static rendering. The data-layer fix is necessary but not sufficient. Tracked as CEO-098.
